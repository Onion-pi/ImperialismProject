My name is Anya, and I am in Ms. Leslie's 10th grade History class. I created this website to share information about imperialism in Morocco.

Morocco has been colonized multiple times throughout its history, and has a unique story to tell about the potential effects of imperialism and how it can influence modern day events.

Go to the various pages of the website to learn about different facets of imperialism and how each one affected Morocco.
